✦ How to Play

  • Draw: Take a known card from the Lobby or risk a blind draw from the Deck.
  • Select: Choose the best patron for the moment from your hand.
  • Place: Seat them in your grid to trigger bonuses (like Aisle seats for Critics or Front Rows for VIPs).
  • Score: The curtains open when the deck is empty. Most Victory Points wins!

✦ Audience Members

Patron Type Winning Strategy
